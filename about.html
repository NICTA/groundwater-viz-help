About

The Groundwater Visualisation System was built to allow easy visualisation and exploration of hydrogeological models and related data alongside authoritative geospacial data.

This is a collaboration effort between New South Wales Office Of Water and National ICT Australia.

This is currently released as alpha for evaluation and feedback.

Further development is subject to industry and stakeholder needs. This system was built on top of the NATIONAL MAP platform.
